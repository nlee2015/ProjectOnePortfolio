Three sources I find for inspiration

Glen Murakami

He was a producer of many super hero oriented cartoons shown on Cartoon Netowrk in the 2000s. His art style incorprated features of anime and other Japanese elements which are shown in the cartoon of Teen Titans. I really loved how those charcters were drawn as the characters felt and looked realistic while still maintaining that cartoon quality. His is art style quickly influenced my drawing style as I started to influence those elements.

San Francisco Neon

This organization restores San Francisco's old neon signs along with preserving the neon history of San Francisco. They help bring new life to old neon signs bringing back vibrance and liveliness into that area where it once lit up which I find inspiring. I think neon signs are very visually appealing as the designs illuminates the night sky which I hope one day my designs can do the same.

OpensfHistory

This organization preserves San Francisco history through their website where there's access to historical photos of San Francisco that everyone can search for. Being interested with the history of San Francisco and even being a former history major, I like to look into the past to find new ideas as I think history and the past influences the present and shapes how we think.
